## Version Notes

### About Version Numbers
This research database uses semantic versioning:
- **Major (X.0.0)**: Significant research restructuring or paradigm shifts
- **Minor (0.X.0)**: New research areas, major feature additions
- **Patch (0.0.X)**: Research corrections, documentation updates, bug fixes

### Release Cycle
- **Daily**: AI agent auto-updates (tracked in commit history)
- **Weekly**: Community research merges and reviews
- **Monthly**: Version releases with curated research summaries
- **Quarterly**: Major research milestones and retrospectives
