### Prevalence and Impact

- **10.6%** of programmers report having ADHD or related concentration/memory disorders
- ADHD programmers are **1.8 to 4.4 times more likely** to struggle with common programming challenges compared to neurotypical developers
- Challenges are especially pronounced in **time management** and **software design** tasks

## ðŸš€ Recommendations for HyperCode

### Language Design Features
1. **Visual Flow Programming:** Node-based coding option for better abstraction
2. **Task Chunking Built-In:** Auto-break large functions into smaller pieces
3. **Persistent Context:** Save exact state, easy resume after interruptions
4. **Focus Mode:** Distraction-free coding environment

### AI Integration
1. **Meeting Summarization:** Auto-extract action items
2. **Task Breakdown:** AI suggests smaller steps for big tasks
3. **Context Restoration:** AI remembers what you were doing after break
4. **Pattern Detection:** AI flags common ADHD pitfalls (e.g., scope creep)

### Accessibility Features
1. **Flexible Deadlines:** Built-in milestone tracking, not hard dates
2. **Gamification:** BROski$ coins for completing tasks
3. **Body Doubling Support:** Virtual coworking rooms
4. **Pomodoro Timer:** Integrated work/break cycles
